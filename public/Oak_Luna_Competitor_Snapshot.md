# Oak & Luna Competitor Snapshot

## Mejuri
Focus: everyday fine jewelry, clean product pages, strong lifestyle photography.

## Pandora
Focus: gifting, charms, emotional storytelling, large promotional calendar.

## Kendra Scott
Focus: color, personalization, gifting and seasonal campaigns.

## GLDN
Focus: handmade, minimal personalized jewelry, emotional product stories.

## Observations
- Competitors emphasize gifting deadlines strongly.
- Personalization examples are very visual.
- Bundles and curated gift guides are common.
- Customer confidence depends on shipping clarity and personalization preview.
