# Oak & Luna Product Launch Brief

## Campaign
Summer Personalization Drop

## Objective
Launch a new collection of personalized necklaces and bracelets focused on gifting, milestones and self-expression.

## Target customer
Women aged 25-45 buying personalized jewelry for birthdays, anniversaries, graduations and self-gifting.

## Key message
Meaningful jewelry made personal.

## Products
- Name necklace
- Initial bracelet
- Birthstone necklace
- Engraved bar necklace

## Risks
- Delivery promise must be clear before gifting occasions
- Product pages need strong personalization examples
- Customer questions may increase around engraving and shipping dates
